Round Hair Brush

Price: $20.00

Directions: For Girls - Take a section down at the bottom of your hair where your shortest layers are and clip up the rest of your hair. The technique when using the brush is really all in the wrist. When you start, you're going to want to take a small section of hair and you'll push up for volume and then out and down for the smoothing effect and your hair/blow dryer is pointed down to smooth the cuticle and when you go up and then out and down the hair/blow dryer simply follows the motion of the brush. Once the section is completely dry, you're going to want to repeat the same process and hold your cold shot button on the hair/blow dryer to seal the cuticle of the hair and really lock the blow-dry into place.

Just keep moving up the section by repeating the same technique up for volume out and down for smoothing and the cold shot to seal the cuticle.

The last section is the top crown area of the head and repeating the same technique at this section is especially important to give it that extra volume. Lastly, smooth the hair out with the round brush just setting in the style and give it a good shake for some volume.
